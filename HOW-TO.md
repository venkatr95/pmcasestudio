# How to Use PM Case Studio 📘

PM Case Studio is designed to guide you through a professional Product Management workflow from ideation to delivery. Here is a step-by-step guide to mastering the platform.

## 1. Creating a New Case Study
1. Navigate to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click **New Case Study** in the left sidebar or the Quick Actions menu.
3. Choose to start from scratch or pick a predefined **Template** (e.g., *District by Zomato*).
4. Select a visual **Theme** to customize the appearance of your deliverables.

## 2. Navigating the 7 Phases
The workspace is divided into 7 distinct PM phases. You can jump between them using the progress bar at the top of your case study workspace.

* **Phase 1: Problem Space** - Define the user problem, target audience, and current alternatives.
* **Phase 2: Customer & Market** - Build user personas, conduct SWOT analysis, and define market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM).
* **Phase 3: Strategy & Metrics** - Establish your North Star Metric, OKRs, and high-level product strategy.
* **Phase 4: Ideation & Solutions** - Brainstorm solutions, wireframes, and technical feasibility.
* **Phase 5: Prioritization** - Rank your solutions using RICE, MoSCoW, or Kano models.
* **Phase 6: Execution & PRD** - Write detailed user stories, acceptance criteria, and Go-To-Market plans.
* **Phase 7: Roadmap & Risks** - Plan the timeline (Now/Next/Later) and identify launch risks.

## 3. Utilizing AI Assistance 🤖
If you ever get stuck, look for the **Sparkle Icon** near text inputs. 
- Click it to generate intelligent, context-aware suggestions for your current phase.
- Review the AI's output and edit it to fit your specific product vision.

## 4. Managing Your Profile & Settings ⚙️
1. Click your **Avatar** in the top right corner.
2. Select **Account Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. Here you can update your Display Name, Email, or establish a new Password for secure sign-in.
4. Changes are saved instantly!

## 5. Generating Deliverables & Exports 📥
Once you've completed your case study, you don't need to spend hours formatting documents.

1. Navigate to the **Deliverables** tab.
2. Under "Generated Documents", choose a specific slice of your work:
   - **Executive Summary** (Phases 1, 3, 7)
   - **Product Strategy Doc** (Phases 3, 4)
   - **PRD Summary** (Phases 5, 6)
   - **Presentation Deck** (Generates PPTX)
3. Or, scroll down to "Export Formats" to download the *entire* case study as a PDF, Word Document, PowerPoint, or Markdown file.

Your selected visual theme (Aurora, Midnight, Minimal, etc.) will automatically be applied to your downloaded PDF and PowerPoint files!

## 6. Admin Portal (RBAC) 🛡️
If you hold the `ADMIN` role, you have access to a restricted dashboard to oversee the platform.

1. **Login**: Navigate directly to `/admin/login` to sign in with your Administrator credentials.
2. **Access Dashboard**: Once logged in, click **Admin Panel** in the left sidebar.
3. **Manage Users**: From the Admin Panel, you can toggle roles (User <-> Admin) for any account.
4. **Review Content**: Click the dropdown arrow next to any user to securely view their case study activity, current phases, and completion statuses.
